Research Interests

The Linked Opend Data cloud is massive, full of implicit knowledge, and unfortunately incomprehensible to humans. Without proper tools to support analyses in these complex environments, many important discoveries will remain latent, a tragic conclusion given the premise that the The whole is greater than the sum of its parts. My research agenda is focused on developing automated mechanisms for conveying the hidden messages trapped in the linked-data cloud, through dynamic and interactive visualizations driven entirely by user requirements.

My dissertation work yielded an extendable approach for generating a wide variety of visualizations. The resulting implementation, known as Visualization Knowledge (VisKo), reasons with knowledge about visualization services supported by toolkits such as Visualization Toolkit (VTK) and Generic Mapping Tools (GMT) to automatically synthesize visualization pipelines.
